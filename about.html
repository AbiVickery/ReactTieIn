GitPony is a website database for all the characters of the show My Little Pony Friendship Is Magic (excluding those from the side show Equestria Girls, the comic books and the merchandise).

On the product page, it shows the ID number, Name, Race, Gender, Residence, Occupation, Family and a picture that has a link to the official My little Pony Wiki Page.

The reason I chose to do a database of this show is because I love to watch it and it has actually helped me to better make new friends as I am extremely socially akward and have a hard time at doing so. In a way, this site is sort of a homage to that.
